Digital-Identity

What is it?

Digital identity refers to the information utilized by computer systems to represent external entities, whether it maybe a person, organization, application, or a device. The attributes about a digital identity are acquired and contain information about the user such as their medical history, purchasing behavior, bank balance, age, and so on.

How does it benefit society?

Digital identity can provide better access to public services and service delivery. Banking/financial services, voting, sharing information can be done more easily online. A digital identity is portable as well as your wallet, no need to carry cash or credentials with you.

What are the consequences/concerns with this trend?

Many low-assurance actions use digital ID, there is more potential for phishing and/or scams that could pose a threat to the digital economy. Another concern is the rise of an authoritarian government that will use this technology to gain social and political control

What components are required for this technology?

A digital identity is made up of data points from the user such as username and password, date of birth, purchasing behavior and history, search history and electronic transactions, social security number, medical history, etc

What are some careers related to this career?

Some careers related to this trend are IAM developer consultants, Software engineers, cyber security specialists, and mobile application developers

Renewable-Energy

What is it?

When someone can use natural energy from the planet (Sun, Geothermal, Wind, etc.) to create cars and other devices without causing Carbon Dioxide to go into the planet's atmosphere.

How does it benefit society?

Creates job creation throughout renewable energy industries. It also allows for abundant, affordable, secure, safe, and clean energy, which is beneficial to humans.

What are the consequences/concerns with this trend?

It may generate toxic substances that may contaminate water resources. Renewable energy installations can also disrupt ecosystems and land use and may use significant amounts of water.

What components are required for this technology?

Batteries and photovoltaic (PV) arrays

What are some careers related to this trend?

Greenhouse Worker, Recycling Worker, Environmental Technician, Urban Planner, Solar Installer, etc

Quantum-Computing

What is it?

Quantum computing, unlike classical computing, is built on Qubits rather than bits. While bits can store a value of one or zero, qubits can store both in any combination simultaneously – this is called a “superposition.” A single calculation leads to a single path in classical computing, but quantum computing can explore a massive number of different paths at the same time due to the volatility of the qubit.

How does it benefit society?

Quantum Computers can solve problems significantly faster than classical computers. In 2019, Google announced that a quantum computer solved a problem in 200 seconds (about 3 and a half minutes) that would take a normal computer 10, 000 years. The vast number of solutions it can come up with can make it easier to develop models of molecules in the human body, accelerate the training of AI system, and do prime factorization. This can help the pharmaceutical companies develop drugs, help the automotive companies teach self-driving cars how to make decisions in crucial moments, and factorize large prime numbers that businesses use as the basis for their encryption efforts.

What are the consequences/concerns with this trend?

One of the main concerns with quantum computing is cybersecurity, especially in the event of large-scale quantum adoption. This means in the geopolitical space whoever gets their first has a huge advantage.

What components are required for this technology?

Some quantum computers implement qubits using electronic states of charged atoms called ions. Trapped ion-based systems apply quantum gates using lasers to manipulate the electronic state of the ion.

What are some careers related to this career?

Quantum Network Security Engineer Quantum Systems Technician Quantum Mechanics Developer Quantum Hardware/Software Engineer

Artificial-Intelligence

What is it?

The ability of a computer or robot controlled by a computer to do tasks usually done by humans.

How does it benefit society?

You can code and program the robots/computers to do things that you need to do so you don’t need to do it.

What are the consequences/concerns with this trend?

There are many diverse concerns regarding AI Technology, ranging from Lack of transparency and privacy to Ethical Dilemmas and Job displacement.

What components are required for this technology?

Some AI components are Speech recognition, Computer vision, and Natural Language Processing.

What are some careers related to this career?

Some careers related to this trend are Machine learning engineers, and AI Product managers, which are the highest paid in that field.

The-Cloud

What is it?

Simply put, Cloud computing is storing and accessing data and computing services over the internet rather than using local hard drives and personal data centers.

How does it benefit society?

The benefits to society from using Cloud Computing is getting their applications to market quickly.

What are the consequences/concerns with this trend?

There are, or rather, lack thereof secure API’s. It is easy to access without authentication, which leads to vulnerability.

What components are required for this technology?

The technology required for Cloud Computing is Client Infrastructure, Application, Service, Runtime Cloud, Storage, Infrastructure, Management, Security, and Internet.

What are some careers related to this trend?

Some careers related to cloud computing are cloud administrator, cloud support engineer, cloud security analyst, cloud network engineer, cloud software engineer, and cloud automation engineer.

Electric-Vehicles

What is it?

Electric Vehicle also known as EV is a vehicle that can be powered by an electric motor which brings electricity from a battery and is capable of being charged from an external source.

How does it benefit society?

Using EV’s addresses an important public health hazard. They can also lower vehicle fueling costs by half, or more.

What are the consequences/concerns with this trend?

EV battery production can adversely impact the environment, such as a loss of biodiversity, air pollution and decreased freshwater supply.

What components are required for this technology?

Head unit ECU Electric Drivetrain Electric Transmission Motors Lithium Battery
